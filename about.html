About

Hello! I'm Frank Mooradian...pronounced Moo-rah-dee-inn, as it’s an Armenian last name. Don’t worry if you mispronounce it. I’ve also accepted what sounds like our globes Prime Meridian. I’ve had a pretty unorthodox journey to design.

Self.edu

I’ve been consistently learning & creating for quite some time. I began teaching myself drums guitar & vocals at the age of 17. I acquired an ACE Personal Trainer certification at 20 so I knew proper form, & finished a fun exercise in teamwork, the Second City Chicago's Basic Improv Program, shortly after.
I stumbled onto Personal Development accidentally, & it changed the trajectory of my life. As the internet matured, there was always SOME way to get my hands on something to learn. Audiobooks, podcasts, Kindle, & Youtube, all contain fantastic content. I'm into many things including philosophy, content from entrepreneurs, biohacking, real estate investing, & every now & then I'll watch an Intelligence Squared Oxford style debate.

Design.edu

Becoming a UX Designer was somewhat of a miracle. My journey through personal development led me to looking at life differently, & to forming a 15 year plan. Education was the first step in my pivot plan, & Bloc's Designer Track nailed all my requirements for a path to a fulfilling career.
The UX problem solving process made absolute sense to me once I fit it all together. It led to me contemplating strategy in life more. User testing & iterating refreshingly matched the pattern of personal development. What I'm most interested in now, is Information Architecture.

Goals.exe

I’m aiming for a holistic Pareto Principle. The more efficient I am inside my career, the more time & mental energy I have on everything else involving creativity & discipline. The fastest way to gain skill seems like learning & teaching my team as a Mastermind Group would do. These unprecedented times have pushed me to focus on remote opportunities, AND have pushed the conversation about remote advantages forward as well. Here's to living to 200!
